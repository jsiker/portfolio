I'm Jon (With No 'H')

Thanks for visiting. Originally hailing from Wisconsin, I've made The Bay my second home. I'm a trained writer and educator and have decided to meld those skills into a career in web development. You can visit my blog about my experiences, or check out my recent projects here.

A few thank you's are in order are in order, namely: Thank you RocketU, the 3 month development program I finished at the end of 2014. Thank you Zed Shaw, whose learning materials and endless advice have helped me down this circuitous path. And thank you to everyone on places like StackOverflow and Reddit, who take tremendous amounts of time and care to collaborate, explain, and teach what they've learned. I'm continually amazed at the spirit of teamwork and camaraderie that is a bedrock of the coding community.

Looking through the lens of web development and technology, the future is truly, intoxicatingly exciting. I'm happy to be able to be a part of it and can't wait for what's next!

What's Now? What's next?

RocketU gave me a strong grounding in Python and Django, Javascript, jQuery, AngularJS, AJAX, PostgresSQL, testing, and deployment.

For me, from a creative background, data visualization was also something that really grabbed my attention. Moving forward, I'm working towards levelling up in my current skills, and expanding my skills with data, from statistical analysis to such staples as D3, Hadoop, and Apache Storm.
